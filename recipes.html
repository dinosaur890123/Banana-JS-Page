Banana Bread

A classic recipe using ripe bananas to create delicious bread.

Ingredients: 3 ripe bananas, 2 cups flour, 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup butter

Instructions: Mix ingredients, bake at 350°F for 60 minutes.

Banana Smoothie

Blend bananas with milk and honey for a healthy drink.

Ingredients: 2 bananas, 1 cup milk, 2 tbsp honey, ice cubes

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth.

Banana Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes with mashed bananas mixed into the batter.

Ingredients: 2 bananas, 1 cup flour, 1 egg, 1 cup milk, baking powder

Instructions: Mix batter, cook on griddle until golden brown.
